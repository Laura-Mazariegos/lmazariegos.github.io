    Upcoming Event:The Continental Divide

    The Continental Divide Event is a 3 day event that covers 203 miles and an estimated 25 hours biking time. This is not for the faint of heart. Riders will covering flat trails as well as mountainous terrain. This event will run the Continental Divide Trail starting at the Homestake Picnic area in Butte, MT and stopping at Thunderbolt Mountain for the first day. The second day will be the longest, continuing on to Alice Creek Trailhead in Lincoln, MT. To finish out the event, the third day will continue on to Sawtooth Ridge, Mt, to be followed up with a celebration and music.

    This event runs $250.00 per cyclist and starts on Thursday May 31, 2018 and ends on Saturday June 2, 2018. Early registration is encouraged due to popular demand.
