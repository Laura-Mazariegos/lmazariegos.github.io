    Adventure Tour- The Scenic Route:
    Come enjoy the fresh country air with the Scenic Route tour. The group will enjoy seeing open fields, farms, and riding on paved roads. This is an entry level tour for beginners to intermedite cyclists covering 40 miles round trip. The mid point of the tour will be at the Montana Grizzly Encounter where there will be a short break before return. The total tour time will be 4 hours with total elevation climbed of 1,473 ft. This tour runs Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays with departure at 8 am and returns at 12pm.
    Register at theeventspage



    Adventure Tour- Saddle Peak:
    This tour will challenge you as we climb 3,727 ft to Saddle Peak. The group will cover 30 miles of leg burning, cardio blasting cycling. This tour is geared for the intermediate to advanced cyclists. Arriving at the peak, a intermission will be taken before return. This tour runs Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays with departure at 8 am and returns at 12pm.
