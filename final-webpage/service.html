Bike Services

  • Basic Bike Tune-Up:Brake & shifting adjustments, Wheel truing (re-tensioning spokes), Check & adjust all applicable bolts & bearings, Tire inflation, Basic wipe-down of bicycle, Check chain stretch, Chain lubrication, Cable lubrication (as needed)
  • Intermediate Tune-Up:In addition to the Tune-Up, we: Remove the drivetrain, Degrease & clean the drivetrain it in our parts washer, Reinstall drivetrain with proper calibration, Clean & polish the bike
  • Complete Bike Overhaul:In addition to our Tune-Up Plus, we: Overhaul Bottom Bracket, Overhaul hubs (wheels), Overhaul Headset.
    This service is recommended for bikes over a decade old
  • Basic Bike Fitting:With the Basic fitting, we will adjust the seat and the handlebars for optimal riding comfort.
  • Competition Fitting:The Competition Fitting will get you ergonomically ready for competition and it's requirements. We will evaluate and adjust your bike using our scanning equipment so that you are riding in alignment. We will set you up with the ideal seat and adjust it as needed along with any shims and arm extensions that would be necessary.
